NLP bridges the communication gap between humans and machines.
It unlocks a vast array of applications that transform various industries.
A few examples include:
Machine translation: Breaking down language barriers for seamless communication.
Chatbots: Providing intelligent customer service and support.
Sentiment analysis: Understanding public opinion and brand perception.
Text summarization: Condensing information for efficient content consumption.
Content generation: Creating human-quality text formats like articles, poems, and code.
