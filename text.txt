**Slide 3: Key Findings from Research**
- The Western concept of nationalism disrupted the Ottoman Empire's traditional millet system¹.
- PSNR and SSIM are widely used in IQA, especially in steganography⁶.
- The rise of deep learning-based methods for non-reference IQA⁹.

**Slide 4: GitHub Repositories**
- "idealo/image-quality-assessment": Implementation of an aesthetic and technical image quality model based on Google's research paper "NIMA: Neural Image Assessment"³.
- "chaofengc/IQA-PyTorch": PyTorch Toolbox for IQA, including LPIPS, FID, NIQE, NRQM(Ma), MUSIQ, NIMA, DBCNN, WaDIQaM, BRISQUE, PI and more¹.
- "chaofengc/Awesome-Image-Quality-Assessment": A comprehensive collection of IQA papers, datasets, and codes⁴.

**Slide 5: Conclusion**
- Image Quality Assessment is a vibrant field with ongoing research.
- Various models are available on GitHub for further exploration and fine-tuning on custom data.
